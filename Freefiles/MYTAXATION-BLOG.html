#1

Old vs New Tax Regime 2026: Which One Actually Saves You More? (With Real Salary Examples)

🔥 Fire Income Tax Filing ~3–5L/month
Volume

Why June: This is perennially the #1 or #2 searched tax topic in India during filing season. With the Income Tax Act 2025 now live, confusion is at an all-time high — new section numbers, updated slab rates, changed HRA city list. ClearTax's page on this gets millions of visits. People need updated examples for ₹8L, ₹12L, ₹15L, ₹25L salary brackets. Angle: include a simple calculator table + which regime is better at each income level.

#2

Form 16 for FY 2025-26: How to Download It, Read Part A & B, and File ITR Without Errors

🔥 Fire Income Tax Filing ~2–3L/month
Volume

Why June: Employers MUST issue Form 16 by June 15, 2026. The moment employees receive it, lakhs search "what is Form 16", "how to read Form 16", "Form 16 Part A vs Part B". Under the Income Tax Act 2025, Form 16 is renamed Form 130 for Tax Year 2026-27 — but still called Form 16 for AY 2026-27 (FY 2025-26), creating enormous confusion. This is a can't-miss topic for June. Cover: download steps, what Part A and B mean, common errors to check, and how to use it in ITR filing.

#3

Income Tax Refund 2026: How to Check Status, Speed It Up, and Fix Refund Failures

🔥 Fire Income Tax Filing ~1–2.5L/month
Volume

Why June-July: "Income tax refund status" is consistently among the top 5 searched tax keywords in India from June through September. People who file early (June) want their refund fast. Cover: how to pre-validate bank account, link Aadhaar, check status on portal, typical processing times, and what to do when refund fails or is smaller than expected.

#4

Capital Gains Tax FY 2025-26: New LTCG & STCG Rates on Stocks, Mutual Funds, and Property — Explained

🔥 Hot Income Tax Filing ~80k–2L/month
Volume

Why June: Budget 2024 changed LTCG to 12.5% (removed indexation on property), STCG to 20%, and ₹1.25L exemption limit. Millions of equity investors and property sellers need clarity when filing. Cover all 3 asset classes — equity/MF, debt funds, and real estate — in one comprehensive post. Include ITR-2 schedule CG filing guidance.

#5

Income Tax Act 2025 vs 1961: 8 Key Changes That Affect Your ITR Filing for AY 2026-27

🔥 Hot Government Updates ~60k–1.5L/month
Volume

Why June 2026 only: This is a one-time peak. The New Income Tax Act 2025 replaced the 1961 Act on April 1, 2026 — but for AY 2026-27 filing, the 1961 Act still applies. This causes massive confusion. Searches like "new income tax act 2025 changes", "new act sections", "Form 16 renamed" are spiking heavily right now. Cover: what changed vs what didn't, section number changes, and what taxpayers must know for this year's filing.

#6

AIS vs Form 26AS vs TIS: What to Check and Fix Before Filing Your ITR 2026

🔥 Hot Income Tax Filing ~40k–80k/month
Volume

Why June: The ITR portal now pre-fills data from AIS. Mismatches between AIS, 26AS, TIS and actual income are the #1 reason for IT notices. Most taxpayers don't know the difference between these 3 documents. Cover: what each statement shows, how to download, how to spot discrepancies, and how to raise a correction before filing. This is a gap no existing blog on your site covers.

#7

F&O Tax Filing 2026: How Futures & Options Traders Should File ITR-3 (Turnover, Audit Limits & Loss Set-Off)

Warm Income Tax Filing ~50k–1.2L/month
Volume

Why June: India has 3+ crore active F&O traders. After SEBI's 2024 F&O restrictions, even more retail investors are confused about tax. F&O income is business income (not capital gains), requires ITR-3, has specific turnover calculation rules, and triggers audit if loss is declared. ClearTax has massive traffic on F&O guides. This is a high-intent audience that tends to hire CAs — direct lead gen for MY TAXATION.

#8

Crypto & Bitcoin Tax in India 2026: How to Report VDA Income in ITR-2 / ITR-3 Under Schedule VDA

Warm Income Tax Filing ~40k–1L/month
Volume

Why June: Crores of Indian crypto investors must report under Schedule VDA. The 30% flat tax, 1% TDS on transfers, no loss set-off rule — all create huge confusion. GST now also applies to platform fees (from July 2025). Searches spike in June-July as filers try to figure out ITR-2 vs ITR-3 for crypto, how to match Form 26AS TDS entries, and whether losses on one coin can offset another. High-value, high-complexity audience for MY TAXATION.
