Our Legacy

High Class Fittings Since 1971

For half a century, NSW has set the benchmark for architectural fittings and small wares in New Delhi. We design and manufacture bespoke steel elements, catering to premium residences, commercial properties, and structural architects.

Every single product is designed, engineered, and manufactured in New Delhi, India. We specialize in using structural grade Stainless Steel (SS) and high-ductility Mild Steel (MS / Iron) to craft fittings that combine security, function, and modern minimalism.

100% Made in INDIA We support domestic supply chains, using pure Indian iron & steel.
Architectural Customization Bespoke home decor, custom safety grills, handles, and small wares.
