Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I secure my outstation booking?

You can book by calling our 24x7 helpline, sending us a WhatsApp message, or filling out the inquiry form. We require a minimal booking advance to confirm the slot, with the balance payable during the journey.

Is airport pick-up available at midnight hours?

Yes, we provide 24/7 premium airport transfer services from IGI Airport Delhi to any destination in Delhi NCR or outstation locations. Chauffeur details are shared 2 hours prior.

Are toll and state entry taxes included in the fare?

Our standard outstation quotes exclude state tax, toll taxes, and parking fees, which are to be paid by the client at actuals on the road. We can also provide all-inclusive quotes upon specific requests.

Is corporate GST billing support available?

Yes. Plaza Tours and Travels is a registered company. We support formal GST invoicing for corporate corporate agencies, schools, and private groups requiring detailed tax logs.
