Step-By-Step Journey

Our Seamless Process

We structure every renovation into five clean phases, ensuring transparent milestones from start to handover.

01

Initial Design Consultation

Deepak and our design managers meet at your site to take measurements, understand your aesthetic choices, and draft a primary project estimate.

02

3D Renders & Technical Layouts

We compile spatial 2D blueprints and high-fidelity 3D renderings of bedrooms, kitchens, and cabinetry. You approve designs before any wood is cut.

03

Material Sourcing & Selection

We visit our partner display experience stores together to touch and select laminate veneers, quartz counter tops, custom lighting profiles, and wall finishes.

04

On-Site Execution & Supervision

Our vetted team of carpenters, electricians, and painters carry out work. Daily supervisor site logs keep you updated on progress remotely.

05

Deep Cleaning & Handover

Following a comprehensive quality checklist, we deep clean the entire space and hand over keys, certificates of authenticity, and warranties.
