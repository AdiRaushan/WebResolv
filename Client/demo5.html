Success Stories

Small Case Studies

Real-world scenarios showing how our proactive solutions resolve compliance bottlenecks and save tax expenses for clients.

NRI Fund Remittance

Hassle-Free India to UK Fund Repatriation

An NRI client sold a residential property in Gurgaon and needed to repatriate sales proceeds to the United Kingdom. Navigating domestic bank requirements and legal certification was a primary blocker.

CA Intervention: Issued Form 15CA & 15CB certifications single-handedly, verifying tax liabilities on the capital gains, and coordinated directly with bank personnel in India to satisfy capital account guidelines.

Financial Impact: Completed the repatriation successfully in 48 working hours, securing a compliant, penalty-free international transfer.

GST Audit & Recovery

₹2.4M Input Tax Credit (ITC) Recovery

A Gurgaon-based logistics operator faced severe mismatch warnings between GSTR-2B purchase reports and input credits claimed, locking substantial operating cash.

CA Intervention: Conducted a meticulous cross-reconciliation of supplier bills spanning 18 months, identified default vendors, and led representations before GST authorities.

Financial Impact: Restored ₹2,400,000 in blocked credit balances within 30 days of audit compilation.

Startup Onboarding

Accelerated Pvt Ltd Setup & Tax Setup

An early-stage SaaS startup required quick Private Limited incorporation to close an equity funding round with seed investors.

CA Intervention: Fast-tracked name approvals, drafted articles of incorporation, coordinated with Ministry of Corporate Affairs, and issued tax IDs (PAN, TAN, GST) concurrently.

Financial Impact: Company incorporated in 5 business days, allowing the client to execute the investment contract on schedule.

ITR Dispute Resolution

Resolving Long-Pending NRI Tax Refunds

An overseas taxpayer had overpaid withholding tax (TDS) on domestic interest payouts, but their refund request was locked by the Income Tax Department due to verification disputes.

CA Intervention: Rectified historical filing disclosures, submitted verified foreign tax residency certs, and represented the dispute before ward commissioners online.

Financial Impact: Resolved the verification discrepancy, securing a prompt direct-bank refund of ₹450,000 + accumulated interest.
