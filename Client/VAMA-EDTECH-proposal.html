Project Overview
01 · Mission Context

Hands-On Educational Mission

VAMA EdTech is a premier learning center specializing in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education, providing exciting hands-on programs for kids aged 4-15 years.

Our creative learning programs include LEGO Duplo Play, LEGO, Paper Circuits, Electronic Circuits, Scratch coding, Robotics, Arduino coding, Python, IoT, and cloud/sensor Arduino programming. We also design specialized workshops and School SUPW programs for Grades 1-10.

By designing a fun, easy-going online explorer, WebResolv will help VAMA EdTech display its interactive curriculum cleanly, building trust with parents and giving them the confidence to invest in their child's hands-on educational growth.

Scope: Kit Store
03 · Science Kit Store Scope

Science Kit Store & E-Commerce

A simple, frictionless shopping setup for parents to purchase physical learning materials and science kits.

Rich Media Previews

Parents view high-res product photos and short experiment demo videos showing the kit in action.

Smart Cart & Offers

Sticky shopping cart that dynamically displays active discount coupons, bundle packages, or special offers.

100% Secure Payments

Razorpay PCI-DSS Level 1 gateway integration supporting secure UPI (PhonePe, Paytm, Google Pay), netbanking, and cards.

Live Delivery Tracking

Parents receive instant WhatsApp/Email updates with active tracking links to monitor shipment status and delivery times.

Parent-Centric Safety Guarantee

By showing video previews of the kits, using bank-grade secure payment pages, and sending instant WhatsApp tracking links, we eliminate parent hesitation, leading to a much higher kit purchase rate.
