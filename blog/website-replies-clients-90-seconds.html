How We Built a Website That Replies to Clients in 90 Seconds

Author WebResolv Editorial
Published May 30, 2026

Most business owners lose leads not because they have a bad product — but because they replied too late. A client messages at 11pm. You see it at 9am. They've already hired someone else. This is the problem we solved for one of our clients. Here's exactly how we built a website that captures every inquiry and sends a WhatsApp reply within 90 seconds — automatically, every time, even at 3am.

The Problem — Leads Were Coming In But Going Cold

Every week, our client was getting 15–20 inquiries through their website contact form. But they were checking email only twice a day. By the time they replied, 40% of those leads had already gone elsewhere.

The issue wasn't the product. It wasn't the price. It was the response time.

Studies consistently show that if you reply to a lead within 5 minutes, your chance of converting them is 21x higher than replying after 30 minutes. After 24 hours, most leads are gone permanently.

The Solution — Website + WhatsApp Automation + CRM in One System

We built a system with three connected parts:

Part 1: The website form
A simple, fast-loading contact form on the website. Name, phone number, and one question: "What do you need help with?" That's it. No 10-field forms that kill conversions.

Part 2: Zapier triggers the automation
The moment someone submits the form, Zapier catches it instantly. No delay. It fires two actions simultaneously.

Part 3: WhatsApp reply + CRM entry
Action one: A personalised WhatsApp message goes to the client's phone number within 90 seconds. It uses their first name, acknowledges what they asked for, and tells them when to expect a call.

Action two: Their details land in a CRM automatically. Name, number, inquiry type, timestamp — all logged. Nothing is forgotten.

What the Client Saw After 30 Days

  • Response time dropped from average 6 hours → 90 seconds
  • Lead conversion rate increased by 34%
  • Zero inquiries missed even during weekends and evenings
  • Client reported spending 2 fewer hours per day on manual follow-ups

The business didn't change. The product didn't change. Only the system changed.

Why This Works Better Than a Human Receptionist

A receptionist costs ₹15,000–₹25,000 per month. They work 8 hours a day. They take weekends off. They forget to log a lead sometimes.

This system costs a fraction of that as a one-time setup. It works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. It never forgets a single lead. It never has a bad day.

We're not replacing people. We're making sure no opportunity ever falls through the cracks while the business owner focuses on actually delivering their service.

Can This Work for Your Business?

This system works for any service business that gets inquiries — real estate agents, clinics, staffing agencies, consultants, interior designers, coaches, or any small business where a missed reply costs real money.

If your website is just sitting there looking good but not actively capturing and following up on every lead — it's costing you clients you'll never even know you lost.
